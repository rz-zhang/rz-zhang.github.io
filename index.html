Biography

I am a 2nd year PhD student at School of Computational Science and Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology. My current advisor is Dr. Chao Zhang, and I am mainly working on text mining and information extraction under his guidance.

Before joining Georgia Tech, I obtained my bachelor's degree (with honors) from the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, where I have also worked under the supervision of Prof. Yong Li in Tsinghua University and Dr. Bo Li in UIUC (remotely) on the topic of spatio-temporal data mining and robust machine learning respectively.

Research Interest

Working with my advisor, I am enthusiastic on building intelligent systems that help people better make use of their text data for task support and decision making. Specifically, I am working on two directions: (1) knowledge extraction from text data in low-resource settings and (2) utilizing knowledge as weak supervision for label-efficient learning. Previously I have also worked on spatio-temporal data mining and robust machine learning topics.
